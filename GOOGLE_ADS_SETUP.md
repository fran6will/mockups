# Google Ads Campaign Setup Guide

Use this guide to copy-paste your assets directly into Google Ads.

## 1. Campaign Settings
*   **Goal**: Sales or Website Traffic
*   **Campaign Type**: Search + Display (Performance Max is also good for visual assets)
*   **Location**: United States, Canada, UK (English speaking)
*   **Budget**: Start small ($10-20/day)

## 6. Audience Segments (Who to target)

### A. In-Market Segments (Ready to buy)
Search for and select these:
*   **Business Services > Advertising & Marketing Services > SEO & SEM Services** (Often overlaps with e-commerce growth)
*   **Software > Design & Creative Software**
*   **Apparel & Accessories > Men's/Women's Apparel** (If targeting clothing brands)

### B. Affinity Segments (Interests)
*   **Social Media Enthusiasts** (Creators)
*   **Technophiles**

### C. Custom Segments (Highly Recommended)
Create a **"New Custom Segment"** and name it "Competitors & Tools".
Add people who browse websites similar to:
*   `placeit.net`
*   `smartmockups.com`
*   `etsy.com` (specifically seller pages if possible, or just general)
*   `printful.com`
*   `printify.com`
*   `canva.com`

### D. Demographics
*   **Age**: 18-44 (Most likely to be starting side hustles)
*   **Gender**: Any

### E. How to Find "Custom Segments"
1.  In the "Audience segments" search box, click the **Browse** tab.
2.  Select **"Your custom audience segments"**.
3.  Click **+ New Segment**.
4.  Name it "Competitors".
5.  Select **"People who browse types of websites"**.
6.  Enter the URLs listed above (`placeit.net`, etc.).
